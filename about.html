About Me

I’m just an ordinary person with a blog, an active life style, and many hobbies. I love traveling to places locally and internationally, both alone and with company. I'd run when I feel stuck, drive an hour to the beach just to watch the sunrise, paint when I feel inspired, sing in the car to stay awake, goof around with friends when stressed, and expand my horizon whenever opportunity comes. I view challenges as chances for improvement and I'm constantly looking for new things to do. I love to go with the flow! Before I could afford a trip or even know how do to something, I’d say yes to it just because having a goal to work towards to is exciting!
